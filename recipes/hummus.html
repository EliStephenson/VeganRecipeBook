Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • Â¼ cup tahini
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Juice of one lemon
  • 2-3 ice cubes
  • Optional: 2-3 tsp curry powder

Instructions

  1. Strain a can of chickpeas, then boil the chickpeas along with the baking soda and garlic.
  2. Strain the chickpeas and garlic and add all ingredients to a food processor. Blend until smooth.
