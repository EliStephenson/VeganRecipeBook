Fruit Pastry/Toaster Strudel

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • Puff pastry dough
  • Vegan cream cheese
  • Vanilla extract
  • Maple syrup or powdered sugar
  • Choice of fruit, sliced (Recommended: strawberries, peaches, or kiwi)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Whisk the cream cheese, vanilla, and sweetener.
  3. Roll out the puff pastry slightly and fold the edges over. Fill with the cream cheese mixture and top with the sliced fruit.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes, or until golden brown.
  5. Note: To make toaster strudels, this only requires a few adjustments. Dice and cook the strawberries on the stove with sugar. Layer a puff pastry rectangle, the cream cheese mixture, strawberries, and another puff pastry rectangle. Then bake the same and top with a powdered sugar glaze.
