Butternut Squash Soup

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large yellow onion, chopped
  • ½ teaspoon sea salt
  • 1 (3-pound) butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and cubed
  • 3 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh sage
  • ½ tablespoon minced fresh rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 3 to 4 cups veggie broth
  • Black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion, salt, and pepper and saute until soft, about 6 minutes. Add the squash and cook until it begins to soften, 8 to 10 minutes.
  2. Add the garlic, sage, rosemary, and ginger. Stir and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds, then add 3 cups of veggie broth. Bring to a boil then reduce the heat and simmer until squash is tender, 20 to 30 minutes.
  3. Blend up the soup. Season to taste and serve with pumpkin seeds, parsley, and bread.
