Crème Brûlée

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 cup silken tofu
  • ⅔ cup granulated sugar, plus more for topping
  • ½ cup thick cream from coconut milk can
  • ¼ cup plant milk
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 vanilla bean or 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ⅛ tsp salt
  • Pinch of turmeric

Instructions

  1. Press the tofu.
  2. In a blender, combine tofu, sugar, coconut cream, milk, nutritional yeast, cornstarch, sea salt, turmeric, and vanilla extract (or inside of vanilla bean, scraped).
  3. Blend and then pour into a sauce pan. Heat it on medium low, whisking constantly, until it’s thick and gelatinous.
  4. Divide into ramekins/bowls and allow to cool to room temperature.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 6 hours.
  6. Right before serving, sprinkle sugar on top and blow torch until caramelized. Alternatively, you could melt the sugar in a pan and pour on top.
