Pumpkin Cream Cheese Muffins

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups flour
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pumpkin pie spice
  • 1 can pumpkin puree
  • ¼ cup melted vegan butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 egg replacement
  • 4 oz cream cheese
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp flour
  • 2+ tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp plant milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit and prepare a muffin tray.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the dry ingredients. In a separate bowl, combine the wet ingredients (pumpkin puree, butter, vanilla, and vegan egg). Add the two mixtures together and stir until just combined.
  3. Fill muffin liners.
  4. Combine cream cheese, vanilla, flour, sugar, and plant milk in a bowl. Spoon or pipe the mixture onto each muffin.
  5. Bake for 18-20 minutes.
