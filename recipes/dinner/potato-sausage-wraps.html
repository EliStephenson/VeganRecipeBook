Potato and Sausage Wraps

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 5 cloves (2.5 tsp) minced garlic
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • Potatoes
  • Paprika
  • Cumin
  • Chili powder
  • Garlic salt
  • Black pepper
  • Vegan sausage, chopped
  • Chimichurri sauce
  • Puff pastry or tortillas
  • Cholula

Instructions

  1. If using puff pastry, preheat the oven to 400 degrees F. If using tortillas, cook those now.
  2. Chop the potatoes into cubes, then heat in the microwave for 6 minutes.
  3. In a pan over medium heat, add oil and the diced onion. Once softened, add the minced garlic. Add the cooked potato and allow them to crisp up.
  4. Add the spices and vegan sausage and cook until the vibes are right.
  5. Fill the pastry or tortillas with filling, cholula, and chimichurri sauce, and cook - 15 minutes in the oven for the pastry or 1 min in the pan for tortilla.
