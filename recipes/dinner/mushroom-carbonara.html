Mushroom Carbonara

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

For Mushrooms:

  • 3 cups sliced baby bella mushrooms
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tsp liquid smoke

For Pasta:

  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 1 tsp kala namak
  • 1 pinch turmeric
  • 16 oz spaghetti
  • Â½ cup vegan parm

Instructions

  1. Marinate the mushrooms in the soy sauce, maple syrup, and liquid smoke.
  2. Bring a pot of water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook until done to your liking. Reserve a cup of water before draining.
  3. Whisk together the water, cornstarch, oil, nutritional yeast, kala namak, and turmeric in a pan over medium-high heat and cook until the sauce thickens, about 3 mins.
  4. Drain the excess mushroom marinade and add the mushrooms to a pan with medium heat. Cook for about 7 mins, until crispy.
  5. Combine the mushrooms, pasta, sauce, and parmesan. Add the reserved pasta water as needed.
