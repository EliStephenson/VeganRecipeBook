Sheet Pan Veggies and Tofu

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 package of tofu, drained and cubed
  • 12 ounces baby potatoes, cut into cubes
  • 2 medium carrots, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 head of broccoli, cut into small florets
  • ½ head of cauliflower, cut into small florets
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp minced shallow
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • ½ cup veggie broth
  • ½ cup vegan cheese, cheddar and mozzarella

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F.
  2. Spread the potatoes and carrots on a sheet pan. Drizzle with olive oil, salt and pepper. Roast for 40 minutes.
  3. After the potatoes and carrots have been roasting for 20 minutes, add the broccoli, cauliflower, and tofu to a second pan and drizzle with olive oil, salt and pepper. Place the pan in the oven and roast for 20 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, melt the butter in a pan and add the shallots and garlic. After about 5 minutes, add the veggie broth, soy sauce, and pepper. Allow to simmer for about 10 minutes.
  5. Remove the baking sheets and combine everything on one pan. Pour the veggie broth mix over the veggies and sprinkle with cheese. Roast for 10 more minutes.
