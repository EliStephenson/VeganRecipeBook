Roasted Broccoli Salad

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

For Broccoli

  • 1 head of broccoli
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp nooch
  • Garlic salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes to taste

For Salad

  • 1 bunch of kale
  • 3 tbsp hummus
  • 1 tbsp nooch
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • ½ of lemon’s zest
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Chop up broccoli (including stem) very small.
  3. Mix up oil, nooch, and spices and pour over broccoli. Roast for 15-20 mins.
  4. While broccoli is cooking, chop up kale very small and mix up the ingredients for the dressing (hummus, nooch, lemon juice/zest, olive oil, salt, pepper).
  5. Mix up broccoli, kale, and sauce until the veggies are well coated. Top with shaved vegan parm, more lemon juice, or fried garlic.
