Palak Paneer

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 block of tofu
  • 10 oz fresh spinach
  • 1 shallot, diced
  • 1 inch fresh ginger, grated
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/4 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tbsp curry paste
  • 1 can tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup veggie broth
  • 1/3 cup full-fat coconut milk

Instructions

  1. Start by browning the tofu in a pan. Season to taste with salt, pepper, and nooch.
  2. Blanch the spinach - boil for about a minute then transfer to an ice bath. Strain the spinach and squeeze out excess water.
  3. Heat oil in a pan then fry the shallots until translucent. Add the garlic, ginger, remaining spices, and curry paste. Stir, then add veggie broth.
  4. Blend the spinach, spice sauce, tomatoes, and coconut milk. Pour back into pan and fold in tofu. Serve with rice!
