Mushroom Soup

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 16 oz fresh mushrooms (ideally a variety, such as oyster, cremini, and shitake)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 8 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp vegan butter
  • Salt and pepper
  • ½ cup dry white wine or veggie broth
  • 1 large sprig rosemary, 2 sprigs thyme, 2 bay leaves
  • 4 cups veggie broth
  • ½ cup cashews
  • 1 tbsp white miso paste
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Fresh chives

Instructions

  1. Slice/tear up the mushrooms. Add the olive oil and onions to a pot over medium heat; once the onions are soft, add the garlic. Add the butter and mushrooms and cook for about 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Pour in white wine and wait for alcohol to cook off, or pour in veggie broth.
  3. Add the remaining veggie broth and the herbs. Bring to a boil, then cover and simmer for about 15 minutes. Transfer half of the soup to a blender and blend up, along with the cashews, miso, and soy sauce. Mix this back into the soup and simmer for another 5 minutes. Remove the herbs and serve with olive oil and chives.
