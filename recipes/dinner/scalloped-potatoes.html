Scalloped Potatoes

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds Yukon gold potatoes
  • 8 leaves of sage (or 2 tsp dried sage)
  • Large rosemary sprig (or 1-2 tsp dried rosemary)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 3 cups full-fat plant milk (or milk + cream, or just milk is fine)
  • ½ tsp nutmeg
  • ⅛ tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 5 tbsp vegan butter
  • 1 medium yellow onion, finely diced
  • 8 large garlic cloves, minced
  • ½ tsp red pepper flakes
  • ⅓ cup all purpose flour
  • ½ cup nutritional yeast
  • 1 tsp dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp white miso paste
  • ½ cup vegan parmesan
  • Handful of chopped chives

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F and spray a baking dish with oil.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp vegan butter over medium heat and add the onion; cook until golden brown.
  3. In a saucepan, add plant milk and herbs/spices. Simmer gently for about 8 minutes. Strain out the big herbs.
  4. Add remaining butter to the onions along with the garlic and red pepper flakes. Add in the flour and then gradually add the milk/cream. Add in the remaining ingredients (except parm and chives).
  5. Layer half of the potatoes in the pan. Top with half the sauce. Add another layer of potatoes and top with the remaining sauce. Add the parmesan on top.
  6. Bake for 1 hour, covered with foil, and 25-30 minutes uncovered.
  7. Sprinkle it with flaky sea salt and chives. Let cool for 15 mins.
