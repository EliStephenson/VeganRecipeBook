Chorizo White Bean Stew

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • Soy chorizo
  • 1 shallot or onion, diced
  • 4 cloves minced garlic
  • 2 tbsp nooch
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Pinch nutmeg
  • 12-24 oz butterbeans, drained
  • Â½ cup plant milk or cream
  • Vegan cheese or more nooch
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • Large handful of greens of choice
  • Fresh sourdough, toasted and buttered

Instructions

  1. Brown the soy chorizo in a pan over medium heat; set aside.
  2. Cook the onions in the pan until translucent, then add the garlic, nooch, bay leaf, and nutmeg.
  3. Add the butterbeans to the pan and leave to simmer for ~5 minutes.
  4. Turn the heat off and stir in the plant milk, cheese, salt, pepper, and greens.
  5. Serve with slices of toasted sourdough bread. Enjoy!
