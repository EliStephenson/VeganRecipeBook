Spicy Crispy Cauliflower

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • ½ of a large cauliflower
  • ¾ cup all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • ¾ cup plant milk
  • 2 cups panko bread crumbs
  • 3 tbsp melted vegan butter
  • 2 tbsp agave, maple syrup, or sugar
  • 4 to 6 tbsp cholula or Frank’s

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit. Line a pan with parchment paper.
  2. Cut the cauliflower into small florets.
  3. Whisk together the flour, onion powder, salt, pepper, and paprika. Pour in the plant milk and whisk until no large clumps remain. Toss in the cauliflower, stirring until every floret is coated.
  4. Place the panko bread crumbs into another bowl. In batches, dip the battered cauliflower into the panko and then spread out on the pan.
  5. Bake for 20 minutes.
  6. Meanwhile, mix the vegan butter, hot sauce, and sweetener.
  7. When the 20 minutes are up, drizzle the cauliflower in the hot sauce and return to the oven for an additional 10 minutes.
