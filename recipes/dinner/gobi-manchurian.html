Gobi Manchurian

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

For Sauce:

  • 3 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp ginger
  • 5 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 1 serrano pepper, chopped
  • 1 large shallot or small onion, diced
  • 6 scallions, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp chili garlic sauce
  • 1 tbsp white vinegar
  • 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 3 tbsp ketchup
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar or maple syrup
  • ½ tbsp cornstarch + 1 tbsp water
  • 1 handful fresh cilantro

For Cauliflower:

  • 1 small head of cauliflower, cut into florets.
  • ½ cup and 1 tbsp flour
  • 6 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp garlic
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • ¾ tsp chili powder
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • ½ cup and 1 tbsp water
  • ¾ cup panko bread crumbs

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Mix the cauliflower batter ingredients together. Coat the florets in batter, then bread crumbs. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes.
  3. Heat a pan with oil until hot. Add the ginger, garlic, pepper, shallot, scallions, and bell pepper. Cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Mix the soy sauce, chili sauce, vinegar, sesame oil, ketchup, brown sugar, and cornstarch slurry. Add the sauce to the pan. Mix until thick and pour over cauliflower.
