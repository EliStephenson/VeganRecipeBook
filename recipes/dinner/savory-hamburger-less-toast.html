Savory Hamburger-less Toast

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • Nice sourdough bread, bagel, or bun
  • Vegan butter
  • Vegan cheddar or gouda
  • Mustard
  • Onions, diced and caramelized
  • Sauerkraut or pickles
  • Veggie dog, if available (IKEAâ€™s are the best)

Instructions for Toast Variation

  1. Slice the veggie dog in half, hot dog style, and cook as desired.
  2. Spread butter on one side of bread and mustard on the other. Place vegan cheese, onions, and sauerkraut on the mustard side. Lay the bread in a pan, butter side down, to toast. Top with sliced veggie dog and enjoy.

Instructions for Bagel Variation

  1. Butter the insides of the bagel and toast in a pan.
  2. Remove from heat and spread mustard on both sides of bagel. Top one side with vegan cheese and sliced veggie dog and microwave until the cheese is melted and the veggie dog is cooked through (you could also cook the veggie dog in a pan or oven). Finally, top with onions and sauerkraut and the other half of the bagel.

Instructions for Hot Dog Bun Variation

  1. Toast the hot dog bun and cook the veggie dog. Slice the veggie dog in half, lengthwise.
  2. Add vegan cheese to the bun and microwave until melted.
  3. Top with veggie dog, then mustard, onions, and sauerkraut. Enjoy.
