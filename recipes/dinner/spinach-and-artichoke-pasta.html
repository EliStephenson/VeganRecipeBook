Spinach and Artichoke Pasta

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 4 to 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • ¼ tsp red chili flakes
  • 1 can of artichoke hearts in brine
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 8 oz pasta
  • 3 cups veggie broth
  • Juice of half a lemon (1-2 tbsp)
  • 2 oz spinach or kale
  • 2 tbsp vegan cream cheese or 2 tbsp vegan sour cream, or 1 tbsp of each

Instructions

  1. Warm the oil in a pot over medium heat. Add the garlic, chili flakes, and black pepper. Sauté for 1 to 2 minutes, until fragrant. Add the nutritional yeast and artichoke hearts and sauté for an additional 3 minutes.
  2. Add dry pasta and mix. Pour in the veggie broth and bring to a boil.
  3. Cook until noodles are tender to your liking.
  4. Lower the heat, then add the lemon juice, spinach, and cashew butter/cream cheese.
