Kale and Brussel Sprout Salad

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • Medium bunch of kale
  • 5 to 10 brussel sprouts
  • ½ cup of hummus (adjust amount to your liking)
  • ¼ cup of lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of mustard
  • 1 tablespoon nutritional yeast
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • Pepper and salt to taste
  • Tortillas
  • Garlic salt

Instructions

  1. Whisk the hummus, lemon juice, olive oil, mustard, nutritional yeast, garlic, pepper and salt to make the dressing.
  2. Massage, fold, and chop the kale into small pieces. Slice the brussel sprouts. Mix both in with the dressing.
  3. To make croutons, cut a tortilla into squares. Drizzle olive oil and season with garlic salt before baking or air frying.
