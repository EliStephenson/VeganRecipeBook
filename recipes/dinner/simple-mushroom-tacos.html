Simple Mushroom Tacos

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 Portobello mushroom or the equivalent amount of baby bella, cremini, etc.
  • ½ of a large bell pepper
  • 6 mini corn or flour tortillas
  • Large splash of soy sauce
  • Large splash of balsamic vinaigrette
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Topping ideas: Pico/salsa, Avocado, Cilantro, Creamy cashew sauce

Instructions

  1. Chop the mushrooms and pepper into strips.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat and add the mushrooms and peppers when it’s hot.
  3. Add the soy sauce, balsamic vinaigrette and black pepper to the pan.
  4. In the meantime, heat up tortillas and prepare extra toppings.
  5. Allow the mushrooms and pepper to cook until most of the liquid has evaporated or been absorbed.
  6. Assemble!
