Caramelized Onion Tofu Sandwich

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 block of tofu
  • 4 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce or coconut aminos
  • Â½ tsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp garlic
  • 1 tsp kimchi sriracha or sriracha
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • Bread or hamburger buns
  • Greens to top
  • Any kind of aioli to top

Instructions

  1. Combine the nutritional yeast, soy sauce, onion powder, garlic, and sriracha to make the marinade.
  2. Slice the onions into rings and the tofu into blocks.
  3. Caramelize the onions in a pan with oil and a spoonful of the marinade.
  4. Marinate the tofu then fry.
  5. Top the toasted bread with aioli, tofu, onions, and greens.
