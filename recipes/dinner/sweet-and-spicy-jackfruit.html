Sweet and Spicy Jackfruit (for jackfruit waffles)

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

For Jackfruit Mix

  • 1 can of jackfruit, drained
  • 2 tbsp agave or maple syrup
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper

For Liquid Marinade

  • 1 tbsp agave or maple syrup
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 to 3 tbsp sriracha
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. After draining the jackfruit, squeeze it with paper towels to get some of the liquid out.
  2. Season the jackfruit with agave, paprika, cayenne pepper, garlic powder, salt and pepper (top ingredients). Add to a pan with oil and fry over medium heat.
  3. Combine the liquid marinade ingredients in a bowl. Pour into the pan with the jackfruit and let it bubble for about 10 minutes.
