Samosa Wraps

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 450 g potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 can chickpeas
  • ½ small red onion
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • ½ inch ginger, grated
  • ½ tsp cumin
  • ¼ tsp chili powder
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp coriander
  • ½ tsp garam masala (or cinnamon + cloves + nutmeg + cumin)
  • Salt and pepper
  • ½ cup frozen peas
  • ¼ cup cilantro, chopped
  • Tortillas (makes apprx 6 small wraps)

  • Ingredients for Cilantro Mint Sauce

  • ½ cup fresh cilantro
  • ¼ cup fresh mint
  • 1 clove garlic
  • ½ inch ginger
  • 3 tbsp vegan yogurt
  • Juice of half a lemon

Instructions

  1. Steam your potatoes and drain your chickpeas.
  2. Over medium heat, add some oil and then the onions with a pinch of salt. Add in the garlic and ginger and then the remaining spices.
  3. Add the potatoes, chickpeas, and peas to the pan. Smash everything together and remove from heat.
  4. Cut a cooked tortilla in half and spoon and flatten a few tbsp of the mixture on top. Fold the round edge over, then fold the whole thing into thirds so it makes a triangle. Cook the wraps on a pan so they become crispy and hold their shape.
  5. Blend the ingredients for the cilantro mint sauce and serve.
