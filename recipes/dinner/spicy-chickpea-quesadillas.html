Spicy Chickpea Quesadillas

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

Flour tortillas

For Cheese Sauce

  • 1 cup raw soaked cashews
  • ½ cup vegan unsweetened yogurt
  • 2 tbsp veggie broth
  • ½ cup salsa
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • ½ tsp chili powder
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 2 tsp taco seasoning
  • 2 tbsp sauerkraut or pickled jalapeno brine

For Buffalo Chickpeas

  • ¼ cup vegan butter
  • ¼ cup Frank’s
  • ⅛ tsp garlic powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • 2 tsp brown sugar
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed

Instructions

  1. Put all the ingredients for the cheese in a food processor and blend until smooth.
  2. Drain and smash the chickpeas.
  3. Melt the vegan butter in a pan and add the hot sauce, garlic powder, salt, and brown sugar. Pour into a bowl for later use.
  4. Next, add the onion and garlic to the pan and cook until soft and fragrant.
  5. Add the chickpeas to the skillet and then add the hot sauce mixture. Bring it to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and simmer.
  6. Assemble the quesadillas!
