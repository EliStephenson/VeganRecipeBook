Lemon Chickpea Soup

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1-2 large shallots
  • 1 tbsp garlic
  • 1/2 inch ginger, finely grated
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 jar chickpeas
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 400 mL veggie stock
  • ~5 potatoes
  • Package of noodles
  • Juice from 1/2 to 1 lemon
  • Greens of choice
  • salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. To save yourself some cooking time, steam potatoes in the microwave. Cut into cubes, place in a bowl with a wet paper towel on top, and cook for 5 minutes.
  2. Slice the shallots then add to a pan with oil. Cook until they are nearly caramelized.
  3. Add ginger, garlic, and turmeric to the pan. Stir then add chickpeas, coconut milk, stock, noodles, and potatoes.
  4. Simmer down until the noodles and potatoes are tender to your liking and the desired consistency is reached.
  5. Add lemon juice, greens, and season to taste with salt and pepper. Enjoy!
