Ingredients

  • Kale
  • Sweet potatoes
  • Craisins
  • Pistachio
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil
  • Tahini
  • Dijon mustard
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Black pepper
  • Avocado

Instructions

  1. Dice the sweet potato(s) and put into a microwave-safe bowl. Cover with a wet paper towel and microwave for about 7 minutes.
  2. Finely chop your kale and drizzle with olive oil. Massage.
  3. Combine the dressing ingredients: olive oil, lemon juice, tahini, dijon mustard, finely diced garlic, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Remove the pistachios from their shells and chop them up. Chop up the avocado(s).
  5. Assemble the salad! Toss the kale, dressing, sweet potatoes, avocado, pistachios, and craisins together.
