Marry Me Tofu

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 block extra firm tofu
  • 1/3 cup flour
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp vegan butter
  • 1 small onion or large shallot
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp dried thyme
  • 4 fat cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/3 cup sundried tomatoes with oil, diced
  • 1 cup veggie broth
  • 1 cup cashew cream
  • 1/4 cup vegan parmesan, grated
  • 2 tbsp fresh basil, chopped
  • salt to taste
  • Rice, gnocchi, or pasta to serve

Instructions

  1. Cut the tofu into shape of choice; I like triangles. Press on pieces with a paper towel to remove some water.
  2. Place the tofu, flour, salt and pepper in a bowl with a lid and shake it up to coat the tofu in flour.
  3. Heat oil in a pan, then fry the tofu until golden brown.
  4. Melt the butter in a pan, then add the onions until soft. Follow with the garlic, herbs, red pepper flakes, and sundried tomatoes.
  5. Pour in the veggie broth, then cashew cream. Let the sauce simmer for a few minutes, then mix in the parmesan, basil and salt.
  6. Serve with yummy sauce and tofu with rice, gnocchi, or pasta. Enjoy!
