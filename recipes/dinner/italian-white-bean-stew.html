Italian White Bean Stew

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 medium shallots, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary
  • 1 bay leaf
  • ¼ tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 can cannellini beans, drained, rinsed, and divided in half
  • ¼ cup creamy plant milk
  • 2 tbsp nooch
  • 1 medium lemon, zested and juiced
  • 1 tbsp miso paste
  • Garlic salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 chipotle peppers in adobo
  • ¼ cup cashews
  • ¼ cup vegan yogurt
  • 2 tbsp vegan sour cream or cream cheese
  • Half of a 28 ounce can of tomatoes
  • 3 cups veggie broth
  • 6 ounces short pasta
  • 1 to 2 zucchini, chopped
  • 1 cup arugula, spinach, or kale
  • 2 vegan sausages (optional)

Instructions

  1. Bring a saucepan to medium heat and add the oil. Once it’s hot, add the shallots and season with a pinch of salt. Cook until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes. Add the garlic and cook for another 2 minutes.
  2. Add the rosemary, bay leaf, and red pepper flakes. Cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes and half of the beans. Cook for about 7 minutes.
  4. Make the white bean puree. In a blender, combine the remaining beans, plant milk, nooch, lemon, miso paste, garlic salt, pepper, chipotle peppers, cashews, yogurt, and sour cream/cream cheese.
  5. Add the veggie broth and white bean puree to the saucepan. Bring to a rapid simmer, then add the pasta, zucchini, and sausage. Cook until the pasta is done, around 10-15 minutes. Remove the bay leaf and rosemary; stir in the greens.
  6. Finish with salt, pepper, or olive oil to taste. Enjoy!
