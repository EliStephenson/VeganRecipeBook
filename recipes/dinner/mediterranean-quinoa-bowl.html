Mediterranean Quinoa Bowl

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 6 falafel balls (or 1 can chickpeas)
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 head of broccoli, chopped small
  • ¼ cup pickled red onions
  • 1 diced cucumber
  • 1 diced tomato
  • 2 diced pepperoncinis
  • 2 cups spinach
  • ½ block of tofu feta
  • ½ cup yogurt
  • ½ cup pesto
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp nooch
  • Garlic salt, pepper, and oregano to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the quinoa in veggie broth, using the directions on the bag.
  2. Microwave the falafel.
  3. Pickle the red onions and chop the broccoli, cucumber, tomato, and pepperoncinis.
  4. Heat oil on a pan over medium heat and add the garlic. After 3 minutes, add the broccoli and saute. When it’s almost done, add the spinach and allow it to wilt.
  5. Mix the ingredients for the sauce - yogurt, pesto, lemon juice, olive oil, nooch, and spices.
  6. Assemble the bowl - place quinoa on the bottom, then top with the sauteed veggies, chopped fresh veggies, tofu feta, falafel, pickled onions, and sauce.
