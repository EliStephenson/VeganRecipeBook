Garlic Bread Grilled Cheese

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp softened butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • Pinch of salt
  • ½ tbsp parsley
  • 4 slices of bread
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Gouda or cheddar cheese
  • Pesto (optional)
  • Chips of any kind (to enjoy with sandwich)

Instructions

  1. Mix butter, garlic, salt, and parsley in a small bowl.
  2. Spread butter on outsides of each piece of bread (don’t forget edges).
  3. Place bread butter side down on low- medium heat pan.
  4. Place optional pesto and generous cheeses.
  5. Place top piece of bread.
  6. Cover pan with a lid to help melt cheese.
  7. Flip when ready.
  8. Cut diagonally.
  9. Munch on a delicious sandwich and maybe some chippies.
