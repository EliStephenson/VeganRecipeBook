Citrus Beet Salad

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 5 medium beets, scrubbed well and cut into slices/wedges
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 2 medium oranges, zested and juiced
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup walnut oil
  • 1/2 cup grated vegan parm
  • 2 tbsp cup nooch
  • Pepper and salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup toasted walnuts
  • 1 bunch purple kale, washed and cut into small pieces

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F. Place the sliced beets on a baking sheet and coat in oil, salt, and pepper. Roast for 35-40 mintues.
  2. Grate and juice the oranges. Add lemon juice, garlic, and red pepper flakes, whisking thoroughly. Slowly drizzle in both oils as you whisk. Toss in the parmesan, nooch, and seasonings.
  3. Mix the kale in with the dressing, and let it sit at room temperature for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Add the walnuts and optionally, orange segments, tossing well. Enjoy!!!
