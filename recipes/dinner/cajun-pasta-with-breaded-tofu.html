Cajun Pasta with Breaded Tofu

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 8 oz pasta
  • 1 tsp oil
  • 3 cloves minced garlic
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • ½ cup sliced mushrooms
  • ½ red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • ¾ cup cashews
  • 1 cup plant milk
  • 1 tsp flour
  • 2 tbsp nooch
  • 1 tsp miso
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 tsp cajun seasoning (paprika, garlic powder, oregano, pepper, onion powder, red pepper flakes, thyme, cayenne)
  • 9 oz firm tofu, sliced
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • ½ tsp mix of sage, thyme, oregano
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp cornstarch
  • ½ cup breadcrumbs

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions.
  2. Marinate tofu in the soy sauce, cornstarch, and herbs for 10 minutes, then coat in breadcrumbs and pan fry until golden brown.
  3. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Cook the onions and garlic and then add in the mushrooms and peppers.
  4. Blend up the cashews, milk, flour, nooch, miso, cajun seasoning, and salt; add to the skillet with the veggies. Bring to a boil, then season to taste. Fold in the pasta.
  5. Serve with breaded tofu and diced tomatoes. Enjoy!
