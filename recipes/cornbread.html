Cornbread

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cornmeal
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ cup oil
  • 2 cups plant milk
  • 2 tsp apple cider vinegar

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit. Grease a 9 x 9 baking dish.
  2. Combine the cornmeal, flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in a large bowl.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix the oil, plant milk, and vinegar. Let it sit for a few minutes.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry and mix just until it's combined. Pour the batter into the greased baking dish.
  5. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, until a toothpick inserted in the middle comes out clean.
