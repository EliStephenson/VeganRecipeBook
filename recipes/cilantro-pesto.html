Cilantro Pesto

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • ½ cup roasted cashews (or pistachios or walnuts)
  • 2 ½ cups loosely packed cups of cilantro leaves and tender stems
  • 2 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 1 small jalapeno pepper, roughly chopped (remove the membranes if you don’t want it spicy)
  • 1 large lime, zested and juiced
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • Black pepper
  • ⅓ cup olive oil

Instructions

  1. Blend the nuts in a food processor or blender until in small pieces.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients and blend. Add water if necessary to thin.
