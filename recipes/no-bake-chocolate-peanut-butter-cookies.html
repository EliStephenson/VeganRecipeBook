No Bake Chocolate & Peanut Butter Cookies

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • ½ cup vegan butter
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • ⅓ cup cocoa powder
  • ½ cup plant milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ⅔ cup creamy peanut butter
  • 3 cups quick oats (not old fashioned)

Instructions

  1. Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine butter, sugar, cocoa, and milk. Stir and cook until it reaches a boil. Allow mixture to boil for 60 seconds without stirring.
  3. Remove from heat and immediately add vanilla, peanut butter, and oats. Stir until well combined.
  4. Scoop mixture onto baking sheets, about 2 tbsp at a time.
  5. Allow to cool in the refrigerator for at least an hour before packing up or enjoying.
