Cheesy Gochujang Noodles

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

For Sauce:

  • 1 cup veggie broth
  • 2 tsp gochujang
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp gochugaru
  • ¼ tsp MSG

For Noodles:

  • 2 tbsp vegan butter, divided
  • 8 oz oyster mushrooms, shredded
  • 1 cup green onion, sliced
  • 12 cloves minced garlic
  • ¾ cup vegan cheddar shreds
  • ¼ cup vegan mozzarella shreds
  • 12 oz udon noodles

Instructions

  1. Combine the ingredients for the sauce in a small bowl. Set aside.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp vegan butter in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add the shredded mushrooms and cook, without stirring, until they start to crisp up, about 6 minutes.
  3. Reduce the heat to medium and add the remaining butter. Toss in the garlic and the white and light green parts of the green onion; cook and stir often for 1 to 2 minutes.
  4. Pour in the sauce and vegan cheese and stir until the cheese has melted and the sauce has thickened.
  5. Boil the noodles according to package directions. Transfer the noodles to the skillet and toss until the noodles are well coated.
  6. Serve with chopped green onion, sesame seeds, fried garlic, and chili garlic sauce. Enjoy!
