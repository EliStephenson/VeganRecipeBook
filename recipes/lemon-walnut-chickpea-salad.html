Lemon Walnut Chickpea Salad

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

For Dressing

  • 2 tbsp walnut oil
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Juice and zest from one lemon
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 2 tsp honey or agave
  • Salt and pepper

For Salad

  • 2 cups arugula
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 2 cups quinoa cooked in veggie broth
  • ½ can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 3 radishes, thinly sliced
  • ¼ cup sun dried tomatoes, chopped
  • ¼ cup walnuts, chopped
  • ¼ cup chopped basil
  • ¼ cup chopped chives
  • Sea salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Mix together all ingredients for the dressing and set aside.
  2. Add the arugula and garlic to a large bowl. Top with warm quinoa - this will slightly wilt the arugula.
  3. Add the remaining ingredients, the dressing, and toss.
