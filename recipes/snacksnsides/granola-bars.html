Granola Bars

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 cup very creamy peanut butter (can substitute other nut butters)
  • ⅔ cup honey or brown rice syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 ½ cups whole rolled oats
  • ⅓ cup mini chocolate chips
  • ¼ cup mix-ins (seeds, chopped nuts, dried fruit, whatever you have on hand)

Instructions

  1. Line an 8x8 baking pan with parchment.
  2. Stir together the peanut butter, honey, vanilla, and salt.
  3. Add the oats, chocolate chips, and mix-ins. Stir to combine.
  4. Press the mixture firmly into the pan, using another parchment paper and the back of a measuring cup to flatten the surface. Store in the fridge.
