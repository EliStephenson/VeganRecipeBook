Spinach Artichoke Dip

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 ⅓ cup soaked cashews
  • 2 cups water
  • ⅓ cup nooch
  • 2 tsp lemon zest + 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp salt
  • Black pepper to taste
  • ¼ cup tapioca starch
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp vegan butter
  • 2 medium shallots, diced
  • 6 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 (14 oz) cans of artichokes, drained and rinsed
  • 8 to 10 ounces of baby spinach
  • ⅓ cup vegan parm
  • Sourdough bread, cut into cubes

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 F.
  2. Blend up the cashews, water, nooch, lemon, salt and pepper. Add the tapioca starch and blend again.
  3. In a pan over medium heat, add the oil, butter, shallots, and garlic and cook for about 3 minutes, until golden and aromatic. Add the artichokes, cook for another couple of minutes, then add the spinach.
  4. Pour in the cashew cream, stirring constantly for a minute. Cook for another few minutes, stirring occasionally, until it has thickened up. Turn off the heat and stir in the parm.
  5. Transfer the dip to a baking dish and cook for 15 minutes. For a golden brown top, broil for an additional 2 minutes.
  6. Serve with bread and crackers. Enjoy!
