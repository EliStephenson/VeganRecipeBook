Zucchini Fries

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 2 small or medium zucchini
  • Ingredients for batter:
  • ½ cup plant milk
  • ½ cup flour
  • 2 tsp mustard
  • ½ tsp salt
  • Ingredients for bread crumbs:
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp oregano

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Cut zucchini into sticks.
  3. Mix the batter ingredients and the bread crumb ingredients in separate shallow dishes.
  4. Dip zucchini into the batter and then the bread crumbs.
  5. Drizzle zucchini with oil then bake for 20 minutes, flipping halfway through.
