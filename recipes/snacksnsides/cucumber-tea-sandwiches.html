Cucumber Tea Sandwiches

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 8 slices of high-quality white bread, crusts removed
  • 1 english cucumber
  • ½ cup vegan cream cheese
  • 2 tbsp vegan mayo
  • 1 tsp fresh minced dill
  • 1 tsp fresh chopped chives
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • ¼ tsp garlic powder
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cut the cucumber into thin rounds with a mandolin or sharp knife. Place them on a paper towel/dish towel and sprinkle with salt to extract moisture. Let them sit for about 15 minutes.
  2. Mix together the remaining ingredients.
  3. Spread the cream cheese mixture onto every bread slice, and layer cucumbers on half of them. Create the sandwiches and cut into triangles or rectangles.
