Protein Bars

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cup almond or oat flour
  • 1/2 cup vegan protein powder
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup very creamy peanut butter (can substitute other nut butters)
  • 1/3 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup soy milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup pumpkin seeds
  • 1/4 cup hemp seeds

Instructions

  1. Line an 8x8 baking pan with parchment.
  2. Stir together the dry ingredients, then the wet.
  3. Fold in the chocolate chips and seeds.
  4. Press the mixture firmly into the pan, using another parchment paper and the back of a measuring cup to flatten the surface. Perhaps top with melted chocolate. Store in the fridge.
