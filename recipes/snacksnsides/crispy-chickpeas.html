Crispy Chickpeas

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 can of chickpeas
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp dried oregano
  • ½ tsp smoked paprika
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • Pinch of cayenne pepper
  • Squeeze of lime juice

Instructions

  1. Drain the chickpeas and lay them to dry in paper or dish towels.
  2. Heat the oil over medium-high heat in a frying pan. Add the chickpeas in a single layer once hot. Cook undisturbed for 2 to 3 mins. Toss and cook until chickpeas are slightly brown.
  3. Add the spices and cook for another 2 to 4 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and top with the lime juice.
