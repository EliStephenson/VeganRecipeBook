Thai Burritos

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • ½ pound vegan ground sausage
  • 2 tbsp fresh grated ginger
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cups of thinly sliced cabbage and carrots
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp honey or agave
  • 2 tsp coriander
  • ½ tsp red pepper flakes
  • Tortillas
  • Fresh cilantro to garnish

Instructions

  1. Add ground meat to a pan over medium high heat and cook for 3-4 mins.
  2. Add ginger, garlic, onion, and vegetables and cook until the vegetables are wilted.
  3. Combine sesame oil, soy sauce, lime juice, sweetener, coriander, and red pepper flakes in a small bowl. Add to skillet.
  4. Serve mix in warm tortillas, garnished with cilantro and, of course, peanut sauce.
