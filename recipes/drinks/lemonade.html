Lemonade

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup maple syrup or agave
  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 6 to 8 cups sparkling water (or regular water)
  • ½ cup mint or basil leaves
  • 12 ounces fresh fruit of choice

Instructions

  1. Blend the fruit, herbs, and lemon juice. Strain the mixture into a jar.
  2. Mix in the maple syrup or agave and top with sparkling water (or champagne if you’re feeling crazy).
