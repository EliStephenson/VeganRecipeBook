Carrot Cake Smoothie

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen banana (if allergic, try zucchini)
  • 1/2 large frozen carrot
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 scoop vanilla or unflavored vegan protein powder
  • 1/2 cup orange carrot juice (with ginger) (orange-carrot-juice)
  • 1/2 cup plant milk
  • optional: 1 tsp honey

Instructions

  1. Simply blend all ingredients and enjoy.
