Golden Turmeric Latte

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp ground turmeric
  • 1 1/2 tbsp ground ginger
  • 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper

Instructions

For Iced:

  1. Stir all spices together!
  2. To serve, mix a tsp of the mix with hot water and a tsp of sweetener. Mix with milk and enjoy!
