Orange Ginger Tea

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1/2 inch ginger
  • 1 tsp to 1 tbsp honey, adjust depending on preferred sweetness
  • juice and zest from one orange
  • 1/2 cup hot water

Instructions

  1. Microplane the ginger.
  2. Thouroughly whisk ginger, honey, juice and a little of the hot water.
  3. Strain, add remaining hot water, and enjoy!
