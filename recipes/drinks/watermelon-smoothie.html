Watermelon Smoothie

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup frozen watermelon
  • 1/4 cup frozen strawberries or raspberries
  • 1/4 cup vegan yogurt
  • 1 cup water or more as needed
  • 2 tbsp lemon or lime juice
  • 1-2 tbsp honey
  • 4 mint leaves

Instructions

  1. Blend all ingredients and enjoy!
