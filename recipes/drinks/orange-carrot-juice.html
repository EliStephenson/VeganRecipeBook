Orange Carrot Juice

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup to 1 cup chopped pineapple
  • 1-2 carrots
  • Juice of one lemon
  • Juice of one orange
  • 1-inch knob fresh turmeric
  • optional: 1-inch knob fresh ginger

Instructions

  1. Crush the spices in the mortar and pestle.
  2. Transfer the spices to a pot. Dry roast for a minute, then pour in the water and bring to a boil for 5 minutes.
  3. Add in the black tea and boil for 5 minutes.
  4. Add in the sweetener and let everything steep for at least 15 minutes.
  5. Strain then enjoy with milk!
