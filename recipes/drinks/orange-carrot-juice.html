Orange Carrot Juice

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup to 1 cup chopped pineapple
  • 1-2 carrots
  • Juice of one lemon
  • Juice of one orange
  • 1-inch knob fresh turmeric
  • optional: 1-inch knob fresh ginger

Instructions

  1. Blend all ingredients.
  2. Seperate the juice from the pulp using a cheesecloth.
