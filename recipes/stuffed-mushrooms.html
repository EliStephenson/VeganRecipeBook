Stuffed Mushrooms

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 package of baby portobello mushrooms, whole
  • ¼ cup grated vegan parm
  • 4 + 2 tbsp panko bread crumbs
  • ½ + ¼ cup prepared vegan pesto
  • ¼ cup vegan sour cream or yogurt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 head of kale
  • 1 pear, sliced thinly
  • 2 tbsp pistachios or nut of choice
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Remove the stems from the mushrooms.
  3. Combine the vegan parm, 4 tbsp of panko, and ½ cup vegan pesto. Stuff the mushrooms with the pesto mixture. Top with the remaining 2 tbsp of panko. Bake in the oven for 8 to 10 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, prepare the sauce. Mix the remaining ¼ cup pesto, sour cream/yogurt, 1 tbsp olive oil, and set aside.
  5. For the salad, thinly chop the kale and wash. Massage it with lemon juice, then pour over a mix of the olive oil, honey, garlic, salt, and pepper. Mix in the sliced pear and pistachios.
  6. Serve the mushrooms atop the salad and drizzle on the pesto sour cream sauce.
