Sesame Udon Noodle Salad

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

For Tofu

  • 1 package tofu, pressed
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce or tamari
  • 2 garlic cloves, pressed/minced
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil

For Dressing

  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce or tamari
  • ¼ cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 1 cup chopped cilantro
  • ¼ cup toasted sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp agave or maple syrup

For Noodles

  • 8 oz package of udon noodles
  • 2 bell peppers, thinly sliced
  • 1 can corn
  • 1 small jicama, thinly sliced
  • 2 scallions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Red chili flakes to taste

Instructions

  1. Slice the tofu and cover in marinade. Leave for 20 minutes to 3 days.
  2. Make the dressing.
  3. Cook noodles according to package directions.
  4. Add oil, bell peppers, scallions, and jicama to a skillet over medium heat. Cook for about 5 minutes before adding corn. Pour over noodles.
  5. Fry the tofu in a pan until golden brown.
  6. Mix the tofu and dressing with the noodles and veggies. Top with any salt and red chili flakes.
