Kale and Tempeh Caesar Salad

Back to Main Page

Ingredients For Salad

  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ cup cashews
  • 1 medjool date
  • 1.5 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp nooch
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • ½ tsp dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper
  • 8 cups kale, finely chopped

Ingredients For Garlic Croutons

  • 2 cups cubed bread
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp each of garlic and onion powder
  • Vegan parm
  • Salt and pepper

Ingredients For Tempeh

  • 1 250 g package tempeh
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ½ cup water
  • 3 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika

Instructions

  1. Combine the ingredients for the tempeh marinade. Thinly slice the tempeh then add to the marinade and refrigerate for about an hour.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Combine the garlic crouton ingredients and bake for 10 to 15 minutes.
  3. Blend all of the ingredients for the dressing. If using fresh garlic (not from jar), roast the cloves at 400 for 30 minutes prior to making the dressing.
  4. Saute the tempeh in a pan with oil, deglazing with the marinade as needed.
  5. Combine the kale, dressing, croutons, and tempeh. Enjoy!
