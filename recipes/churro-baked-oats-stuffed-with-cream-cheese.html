Churro Baked Oats Stuffed with Cream Cheese

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • ½ a ripe banana
  • ½ cup oats
  • ⅓ cup plant milk
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tbsp ground flax or other egg replacement
  • 2 tsp agave
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • ¼ tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp cream cheese
  • Cinnamon sugar to sprinkle on top

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Blend all of the ingredients, except the cream cheese and cinnamon sugar.
  3. Pour ¾ of the batter into a small oven-safe dish. Add the cream cheese and then top with the remaining batter.
  4. Bake for 22-30 minutes.
  5. Top with cinnamon sugar.
