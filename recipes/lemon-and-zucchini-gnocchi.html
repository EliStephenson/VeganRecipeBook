Lemon & Zucchini Gnocchi

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 package of gnocchi
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 2 large, diced zucchini
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • ⅔ cup vegan parm
  • ⅔ cup roughly chopped basil
  • Garlic salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil. Salt generously and add the gnocchi.
  2. Meanwhile, melt 2 tbsp of butter in a pan and add the zucchini. Cook for about 5 minutes, then add the garlic.
  3. Once the gnocchi float to the top of the water, strain them and add to the pan with the zucchini and garlic. Add the remaining butter, lemon juice, parm, basil, and about ¼ cup of the gnocchi water.
  4. Season to taste and enjoy.
