Granola

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole rolled oats
  • ½ cup chopped nuts
  • ½ cup coconut flakes
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp sea salt
  • 2 tbsp melted coconut oil
  • ¼ cup maple syrup
  • 2 tbsp creamy nut butter
  • ⅓ cup dried cranberries or goji berries

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 300 F and line a baking sheet with parchment.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the oats, nuts, coconut flakes, cinnamon, and salt. Drizzle in the coconut oil, maple syrup, and nut butter. Stir until combined. Scoop the mixture onto the baking sheet and press the mixture into a 1-inch-thick oval to encourage clumping.
  3. Bake for 30 minutes, rotating the pan halfway. Sprinkle with dried fruit, let cool, and enjoy.
  4. Note: This is totally customizable. You could use peanut butter, almond butter, walnuts, almonds, macadamia nuts, etc.
