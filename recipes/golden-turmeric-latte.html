Golden Turmeric Latte

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plant milk
  • 1 tsp to 1 tbsp maple syrup, adjust depending on preferred sweetness
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 to 2 tsp fresh grated ginger
  • ⅛ tsp cinnamon
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ tsp coconut oil
  • Optional Additions:
  • Pinch black pepper
  • Star anise
  • Pinch cayenne pepper
  • Pinch nutmeg

Instructions

For Iced:

  1. Froth all ingredients with a tiny bit of boiling water.
  2. Pour milk over ice and add the turmeric mix.
  3. Stir and top with cinnamon!

For Hot:

  1. Mix all ingredients in a saucepan and pour into a mug.
  2. OR warm up milk in the microwave and froth ingredients together.
  3. Top with cinnamon.
