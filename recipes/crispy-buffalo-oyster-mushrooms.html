Crispy Buffalo Oyster Mushrooms

Back to Main Page

Ingredients for batter

  • ½ cup plant milk
  • ½ cup water
  • ¾ cup flour
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper

Ingredients for breading

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp ground sage
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp all spice
  • ½ tsp cumin

Instructions

  1. Combine the batter ingredients in a large bowl. Pour half of the batter into another bowl with the mushrooms. Let sit for about 20 minutes.
  2. Heat up the oil in a deep pan or pot to 355 to 360 degrees Fahrenheit.
  3. Combine the breading ingredients. Take the mushrooms out of the batter, coat in the breading and then add to the other bowl of batter. Then add to the frying oil.
  4. Fry mushrooms until golden brown and drain on a wire rack. Don’t use paper towels.
  5. Drizzle buffalo sauce over the mushrooms and enjoy as a sandwich with ranch and coleslaw.
