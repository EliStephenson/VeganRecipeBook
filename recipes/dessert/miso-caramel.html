Ingredients

  • 2.5 tbsp softened vegan butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp white miso paste
  • 3-5 tbsp plant milk

Instructions

  1. Soak the dates in hot water for at least 10 minutes.
  2. Blend all ingredients, adding the plant milk a little at a time until your desired consistency is reached.
