Blueberry Lavender Scones

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp dried culinary lavender
  • 1 tbsp ground flax seeds
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ cup cold vegan butter (or solid coconut oil)
  • ½ cup almond milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ¾ cup fresh or frozen blueberries
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tbsp milk, lemon juice, orange juice, or lavender syrup

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F and line a baking sheet with parchment.
  2. Blend up the flaxseeds and lavender, if they are not already ground.
  3. Whisk together the flour, baking powder, flaxseeds, lavender, salt, and granulated sugar.
  4. Add the butter. Use your hands or a pastry cutter to work it into the dry ingredients. It should resemble coarse crumbs.
  5. Pour in the milk and vanilla, stirring until just combined. Add up to ¼ cup more milk if the dough seems too dry. Fold in the blueberries.
  6. On a lightly floured surface, knead the dough until it comes together. Flatten the dough into a circle, about 1 inch thick.
  7. Using a pizza cutter or knife, cut it like you would a pizza, into 8 pieces.
  8. Bake the scones for 22 to 26 minutes, until lightly golden brown on top.
  9. To make the glaze, simply whisk the powdered sugar with your desired liquid (lemon juice, orange juice, lavender syrup, or just milk).
