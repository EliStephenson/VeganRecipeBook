Thumbprint Cookies

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups vegan butter, softened
  • ⅛ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla or almond extract
  • 1/2 cup filling of choice (jam, lemon curd, caramel, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F and line two baking sheets with parchment.
  2. Stir the flour and powdered sugar then mix in the butter, salt, and extract with an electric mixer. Use your hands to smush the dough together if needed.
  3. Shape the dough into ~1 tbsp balls, place on baking sheet, and make a small indentation with your finger in the center of each cookie. Then, fill with filling.
  4. Freeze for 10 minutes then bake for 15.
  5. Let cool for a few minutes on the pan then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely. Enjoy!
