Thumbprint Cookies

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp sugar or sweetener
  • ⅛ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp oil or melted vegan butter
  • 3 tbsp plant milk
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • Optional: large spoonful of chocolate chips, peanut butter, or protein powder

Instructions

  1. Whisk the dry ingredients in a mug.
  2. Mix in the wet ingredients and optional ingredients.
  3. Cook for 40 seconds to a minute, depending on how gooey you like it.
