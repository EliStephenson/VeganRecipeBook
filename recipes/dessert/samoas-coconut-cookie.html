Samoas (Coconut Cookies)

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ cups unsweetened coconut shreds
  • ¼ cup almond flour
  • 3 tbsp solid coconut oil
  • ¼ cup maple syrup
  • Miso caramel for topping
  • Chocolate chips for topping

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Line a baking sheet with parchment.
  2. Add all ingredients (coconut, flour, coconut oil, maple syrup) to a blender or food processor. Blend until you get a thick and sticky mixture.
  3. Form the dough into balls and drop onto the cookie sheet. Bake for 12-16 minutes, until golden on the edges.
  4. Spoon a thin layer of miso caramel onto each cookie, or drizzle.
  5. Melt a small amount of chocolate chips (1-2 tbsp) and drizzle onto the cookies.
