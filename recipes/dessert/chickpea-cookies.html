Chickpea Cookies

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • ½ cup creamy peanut butter
  • ⅓ cup maple syrup
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1-2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • pinch of salt
  • ¼ cup vegan chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F and line a baking pan with parchment.
  2. Blend up the chickpeas, peanut butter, maple syrup, vanilla, and cocoa powder until completely smooth.
  3. Add the baking powder and baking soda and blend until just incorporated. Over blending will result in flat cookies.
  4. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  5. Form into cookie shapes and bake for 18-20 minutes.
