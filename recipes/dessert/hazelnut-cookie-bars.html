Hazelnut Cookie Bars

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups hazelnuts
  • 1 ¼ cup rolled oats
  • 1 packed cup soft, pitted medjool dates
  • ⅓ cup cocoa powder
  • ½ tsp sea salt
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp melted coconut oil
  • ⅓ cup maple syrup
  • ½ cup melted chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375. Line a pan with parchment paper and spread out the hazelnuts evenly. Roast for 10-15 minutes.
  2. Turn the oats into oat flour in the food processor; set aside.
  3. Once the hazelnuts are done roasting, cover them with a paper towel and rub off the skins. It’s okay if you don’t get them all.
  4. Grind up the hazelnuts in the food processor until they are finely ground. Don’t grind them for too long or you will end up with hazelnut butter!
  5. Add the remaining ingredients (except melted chocolate) until you have a sticky dough.
  6. Spread the dough out on a pan, freeze for 10-30 minutes, then spread on the melted chocolate. They will keep well in the freezer for several weeks!
