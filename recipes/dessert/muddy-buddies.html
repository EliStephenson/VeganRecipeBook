Muddy Buddies

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 7 cups rice chex cereal
  • 1 1/2 cups vegan chocolate chips
  • Â¾ cup creamy peanut butter
  • 6 tbsp vegan butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 to 4 cups of powdered sugar

Instructions

  1. Add the cereal to a large bowl; set aside.
  2. In a microwave safe bowl, add the chocolate chips, peanut butter, and butter. Microwave in 30 second intervals until melted and smooth. Stir in the vanilla.
  3. Pour the mixture over the cereal and use a spatula to stir gently.
  4. Add the powdered sugar and use the spatula again to coat all of the sugar.
