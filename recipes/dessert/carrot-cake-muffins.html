Carrot Cake Muffins

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

For Muffins:

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 3 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp ginger, ground
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp plant milk
  • 1/2 cup melted vegan butter or neutral oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups grated carrots and/or pulp from ginger carrot juice
  • 3/4 cup chopped walnuts

For Cream Cheese Filling/Topping:

  • 4 oz cream cheese
  • Â½ tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp flour
  • 2+ tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp plant milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit and prepare a muffin tray. You can also make donuts; just spray a donut pan with oil.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the dry ingredients. In a separate bowl, combine the milk and ACV to curdle. After 5 mins, add the remaining ingredients. Add the two mixtures together and stir until just combined.
  3. Fill muffin liners/donut pan.
  4. Combine cream cheese, vanilla, flour, sugar, and plant milk in a bowl. Spoon or pipe the mixture onto each muffin.
  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes. Enjoy!
