Snickerdoodles

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • ½ cup vegan butter, softened
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp plant milk
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ cups flour
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • Cinnamon Coating:
  • ⅛ cup sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Use a mixer to mix the butter, sugar and brown sugar together until it’s light and fluffy. Add plant milk and vanilla and mix again.
  3. Add the dry ingredients and stir.
  4. Roll the dough into balls and coat in the cinnamon-sugar mix before putting on baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 12 minutes.
