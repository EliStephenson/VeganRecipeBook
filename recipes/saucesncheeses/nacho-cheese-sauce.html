Nacho Cheese Sauce

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup peeled and diced gold potato
  • ¾ cup peeled and diced sweet potato or carrots
  • ¼ cup raw cashews
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1-2 tbsp liquid from sauerkraut or pickled jalapeños
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • ½ tsp onion powder
  • ½ tsp sea salt
  • 2-3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • ¼ cup water or enough to thin

Instructions

  1. Place the potatoes/carrots and cashews in a pot of water and boil, then reduce the heat and simmer until fork-tender.
  2. Drain and transfer to a blender with the remaining ingredients. Blend until smooth.
