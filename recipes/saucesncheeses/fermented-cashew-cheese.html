Fermented Cashew Cheese

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cup raw cashews, soaked for at least 8 hours (or boiled 30 minutes)
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar with “the mother” (shake to distribute)
  • 1 to 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 3 tbsp water, may need more if your blender isn’t powerful
  • ⅓ cup coconut oil
  • ½ cup nutritional yeast
  • ¾ tsp garlic powder ***NOT fresh garlic
  • ¾ tsp onion powder
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Blend all of the ingredients until well incorporated and smooth.
  2. Pour into a clean glass jar with an airtight lid.
  3. Let the jar ferment in a warm place for 16 to 48 hours. This could be the oven with the lightbulb on, your countertop, or outside if it’s not too hot. The warmer the environment, the less time is needed to ferment.
  4. After fermentation, give it a stir and refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
  5. Once it has solidified, spoon onto a parchment paper and use the paper to roll it into a log shape. Refrigerate for another 2 hours or more.
