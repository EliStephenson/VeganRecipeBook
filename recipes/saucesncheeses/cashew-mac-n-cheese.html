Cashew Mac and Cheese Sauce

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • Soaked cashews
  • Veggie broth
  • Onion powder or cooked onions
  • Garlic powder or cooked garlic
  • Sauerkraut liquid
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Vegan yogurt
  • Nutritional yeast

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients in a blender. Start with just a little veggie broth and then add more until you reach your desired consistency.
  2. Pour over cooked elbow pasta and enjoy.
