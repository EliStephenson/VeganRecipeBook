Cheddar

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (150 grams) raw cashews
  • 2-4 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp sundried tomatoes, with oil
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1 tbsp miso paste
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tbsp agar agar powder

Instructions

  1. Blend all ingredients except the 1 cup water and agar agar.
  2. Mix the agar agar and water in a small pot on a stove. Bring to a boil, then let it boil for one minute.
  3. Transer the agar mixture to the blender and blend with the rest of the ingredients.
  4. Working quickly, transfer the cheese to a container. Let it set in the fridge for an hour or so, then enjoy!
