Chimichurri Sauce

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 cup firmly packed fresh parsley
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp fresh oregano or 2 tsp dried oregano
  • ⅓ cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp red or white wine vinegar
  • ½ tsp sea salt
  • ⅛ tsp black pepper
  • ¼ tsp red pepper flakes

Instructions

  1. Finely chop or food process the parsley, garlic, and oregano.
  2. Stir in the remaining ingredients.
