  • 2 red bell peppers

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3-4 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 1-2 tbsp lemon juice
  • Red pepper flakes to taste
  • Garlic salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit and soak the cashews in hot water.
  2. Roughly chop the bell peppers, onion, and garlic. Place on a baking sheet and coat in oil. Sprinkle salt and pepper on top. Roast in the oven for 20-30 minutes.
  3. Combine the roasted veggies, cashews, olive oil, nooch, and spices in a blender and blend until smooth.
  4. Serve over pasta with vegan parm, sausage, and a dollop of pesto. Alternatively, mix up with chickpeas and serve on toast.
