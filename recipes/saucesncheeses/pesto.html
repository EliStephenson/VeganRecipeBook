Ingredients

  • 2 cups packed fresh basil
  • 3 tbsp pine nuts or walnuts
  • 3 large garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup nooch or vegan parm
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 3 garlic cloves grated
  • 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2-4 tbsp water

Instructions

  1. In a mortar and pestle or a small blender, add the basil, nuts, garlic, lemon juice, nooch, and salt.
  2. Add olive oil, then water, one tablespoon at a time until your desired consistency is reached!
