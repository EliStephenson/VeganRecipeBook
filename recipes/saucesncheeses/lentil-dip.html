Lentil Dip

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • Cooked Lentils
  • Salsa
  • Taco seasoning
  • Cumin
  • Hot sauce
  • Diced Bell Peppers
  • Diced Onions
  • Chopped/diced Garlic
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the lentils.
  2. Combine everything in a small pot and bring to a simmer.
  3. Serve over nachos, in tacos, or just use as a dip.
