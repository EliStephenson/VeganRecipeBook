Stovetop Mac and Cheese Sauce

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • A little Coconut / oat milk
  • Flour
  • Nutritional yeast
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Paprika
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Yogurt
  • Lemon juice
  • Liquid from sauerkraut jar
  • Vegan shredded Cheese

Instructions

  1. Start by creating a roux with the flour and butter over low to medium heat.
  2. Toss in the spices, nutritional yeast, butter, mustard, yogurt, lemon juice, and sauerkraut juice, tasting and adjusting ratios as you go.
  3. Once the sauce is well combined, add the shredded vegan cheese and mix to melt.
  4. Finally, add cooked elbow pasta. I prefer to add a little pasta at a time so that we don’t end up with a mac and cheese that isn’t cheesy enough.
