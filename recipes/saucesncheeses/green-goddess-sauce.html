Green Goddess Sauce

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 1 garlic bulb
  • 10 g fresh chives
  • 10 g fresh parsley
  • 10 g fresh basil
  • 300 g vegan yogurt
  • 300 g vegan cream cheese
  • 1 tbsp dijon mustard
  • 2 tsp agave
  • Juice from 2 lemons
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Peel and roughly chop the onion and garlic.
  2. Wash the herbs.
  3. Combine all ingredients in a blender until smooth.

Note

This is yummy on pasta with toasted sunflower seeds, vegan parmesan, and sauteed edamame, zucchini, and sugar snap peas.
