Ingredients

  • 1 cup raw soaked cashews
  • ½ cup vegan unsweetened yogurt
  • 2 tbsp veggie broth
  • ½ cup salsa
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • ½ tsp chili powder
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 2 tsp taco seasoning
  • 2 tbsp sauerkraut or pickled jalapeno brine

Instructions

  1. Put all the ingredients for the cheese in a food processor and blend until smooth.
