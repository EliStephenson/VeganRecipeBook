Crispy Smashed Potatoes

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • Baby potatoes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp melted butter
  • Garlic salt
  • Pepper
  • Optional for garnish: parsley, chives, flakey sea salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Cook the potatoes in the microwave for about 5 minutes, covered with a wet paper towel.
  3. Add the potatoes to the baking sheet and smash them with a mason jar or can.
  4. Drizzle the potatoes with oil and butter and top with spices.
  5. Cook for 35 minutes.
