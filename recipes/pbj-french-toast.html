PB & J French Toast

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 8 slices bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jam
  • Ingredients for creamy mix:
  • Â½ cup plant milk
  • 3 tbsp flour
  • 1 tbsp syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Ingredients for cinnamon mix:
  • 4 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • Pinch salt

Instructions

  1. To make the bread stale, bake it at 400 degrees F for 10 minutes.
  2. Whisk the ingredients for the creamy mix in a shallow dish.
  3. Make pb&j sandwiches and cut each one into 3 strips.
  4. Heat vegan butter in a pan. Dip the sandwich sticks in the creamy mix and transfer to the pan. Fry over medium heat for 4-5 minutes.
  5. Sprinkle the sticks with cinnamon mix and serve with berries, whipped cream, or syrup!
