Veggie Potstickers

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white onion
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp ginger
  • 1-2 cups mushrooms
  • 1 cup cabbage
  • 1 cup carrots
  • 2-3 tbsp soy sauce
  • Â½ tsp agave
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp toasted sesame seeds
  • Approximately 20 wonton wrappers

Instructions

  1. Chop up the onions, garlic, ginger, mushrooms, cabbage, and carrots.
  2. Add oil to a pan over medium-high heat. Add the onion, ginger, and garlic; saute until fragrant.
  3. Add remaining veggies and cook until liquid has evaporated, about 8 minutes.
  4. Stir together the soy sauce, agave, and oil and pour onto veggies.
  5. Stir in sesame seeds.
  6. Fill the wonton wrappers with the veggies and fry in a pan (or air fryer or steam fry).

To serve: Top with green onions, sesame seeds, and a fun sauce.
