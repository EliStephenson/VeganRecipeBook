Lemon Poppy Seed Muffins

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plant milk
  • 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 ½ tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp poppy seeds
  • ⅔ cup sugar
  • 5 tbsp olive oil
  • Zest and juice of 2 lemons
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • For glaze: ½ cup powdered sugar, 2-3 tsp lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375.
  2. Combine apple cider vinegar and milk; let sit for 10 minutes.
  3. Mix the dry ingredients (except sugar), then separately mix the curdled milk, sugar, oil, vanilla extract, lemon juice, and lemon zest.
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet.
  5. Fill muffin molds with batter and bake for 20 minutes.
  6. In the meantime, whisk together the ingredients for the glaze. When the muffins are done, spoon glaze on top!
