Stuffed Sweet Potato

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 sweet potato
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 fatty garlic cloves
  • ½ tsp cumin
  • ¼ tsp chili powder
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • Salt and pepper
  • ¼ cup salsa
  • ½ of an avocado or guacamole
  • Sprinkling of nutritional yeast
  • Cilantro

Instructions

  1. Poke holes in the sweet potato with a fork. Wrap in a wet paper towel and microwave until soft, 5 to 10 minutes.
  2. Cook the diced onion for 5 mins on a pan with a little olive oil, then add the garlic, spices, and black beans and cook for a few more minutes.
  3. Turn off the heat and add lime juice.
  4. Fluff the sweet potato and add salt.
  5. Top with black beans, salsa, nutritional yeast, avocado and cilantro.
