Creamy Orzo

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 2 shallots or 1 onion, diced
  • 2 tsp garlic
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp fresh rosemary
  • 1.5 cups orzo
  • 4 cups veggie broth
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ⅛ tsp white pepper
  • ¼ tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp onion powder
  • Salt to taste
  • ½ cup cashews
  • ¼ cup Water
  • 2 tbsp Nooch
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Vegan Parm

Instructions

  1. Add olive oil to a pan over medium heat. Once hot, add the onion/shallot and cook until translucent. Then, add the garlic, honey, and rosemary, cooking until fragrant.
  2. Add the dry orzo, stir, and then add the veggie broth. Season with the peppers, paprika, onion powder, and salt. Cook until the orzo is done and the liquid has significantly reduced.
  3. Meanwhile, blend cashews, water, nooch, and lemon juice. Add to the orzo once it’s done cooking.
  4. Top with vegan parm and enjoy!
