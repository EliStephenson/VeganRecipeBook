Avocado Cilantro Dressing

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 200 g (7 oz) vegan mayo
  • ½ avocado
  • 15 g (0.5 oz) fresh cilantro
  • ½ lime
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Peel the avocado and remove the pit.
  2. Squeeze the lime juice into a bowl. Add the remaining ingredients.
  3. Puree in a blender/food processor, or whisk together.
