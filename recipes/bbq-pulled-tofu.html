BBQ Pulled Tofu

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • Block of tofu
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Press and shred a block of tofu.
  2. Whisk the rest of the ingredients together, then mix in the shredded tofu.
  3. Bake at 350 degrees F for 28-33 minutes, flipping once.
