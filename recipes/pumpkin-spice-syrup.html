Pumpkin Spice Syrup

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup pumpkin puree
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tsp pumpkin pie spice

Instructions

  1. Whisk all the ingredients together in a saucepan over high heat. Bring it to a boil.
  2. Lower the heat to medium and leave until it appears oily and glossy.
