Mushroom Banh Mi

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 4 King Oyster Mushrooms
  • 1 tsp oil
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 minced onions
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • ¼ tsp five spice
  • ⅛ tsp chili powder
  • ¼ block thinly sliced tofu
  • 1 small thinly sliced cucumber
  • Cilantro
  • 2 small baguettes

    • For Pickled Carrots:

  • One thinly sliced or shredded carrot
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp white vinegar
  • 3 tbsp water

Instructions

  1. To make the pickled carrots, dissolve the sugar and salt in the water and vinegar. Add the shredded carrots and let them sit for at least an hour.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. Wash and dry the mushrooms, then shred them. Transfer them to the baking sheet and drizzle with the oil. Bake for 20 minutes.
  4. Fry thinly sliced tofu in a pan with a little oil.
  5. Mix the soy sauce, sugar, onions, garlic, five spice, and chili powder in a bowl.
  6. Add the mushrooms and spice mix to the pan with the tofu and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  7. Slice and warm the bread, drain the pickled carrots, and arrange the sandwiches with the carrots, cucumbers, mushroom tofu mix, cilantro and spicy mayo!
