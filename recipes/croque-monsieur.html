Croque Monsieur

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp flour
  • 3 tbsp vegan butter
  • 1 cup plant milk
  • ¼ cup vegan cream
  • 1 tsp dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • ½ cup vegan grated cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Nutmeg
  • 8 slices bread
  • 4 slices vegan cheese
  • Package of vegan deli meat

Instructions

  1. For your bechamel sauce, melt vegan butter in a saucepan and stir in flour carefully, so it doesn't get clumpy. Let it heat for 1 minute, then add soy milk gradually. Add soy cream, vegan grated cheese, mustard, nutritional yeast, salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
  2. Transfer half of the sliced bread onto a parchment-lined baking sheet. Spread some vegan butter on each slice and top it with vegan ham, bechamel-cheese mixture, and some vegan cheese slices. Cover with remaining sliced bread and add some more vegan bechamel-cheese mixture on top.
  3. Bake at 390 degrees F for 10 minutes.
