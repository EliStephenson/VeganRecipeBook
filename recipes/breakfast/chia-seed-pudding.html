Chia Seed Pudding

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup plant milk
  • 2 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tsp maple syrup
  • 1/8 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Instructions

  1. Shake the ingredients in a jar and chill for a few hours. Give it a stir/shake, then continue chilling for 8 hours.
  2. Note: serve with fruit of choice, nut butter, and homemade granola. Yum!
