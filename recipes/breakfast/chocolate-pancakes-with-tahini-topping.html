Chocolate Pancakes with Tahini Topping

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (125 g) flour
  • 2 tbsp (15 g) cocoa powder
  • ¼ cup (50 g) brown sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 cup plant milk
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar

Toppings

  • ⅓ cup tahini
  • ½ cup plant-based yogurt
  • ¼ cup agave or maple syrup
  • 2 tbsp sesame seeds
  • Fresh fruits

Instructions

  1. Mix the apple cider vinegar and the plant milk; let sit for 5 minutes.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix all the dry ingredients.
  3. Add the milk to the dry ingredients and stir. Let rest for 10 minutes.
  4. In a small bowl, mix the tahini, yogurt, and syrup together to make the tahini syrup.
  5. Heat an oiled pan over medium low heat. Pour ¼ cup of batter into the pan for each pancake; cook until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Serve the pancakes with the tahini syrup, sesame seeds, and fresh fruit (bananas, berries, etc).
