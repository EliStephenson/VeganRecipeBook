Pancakes

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1 cup plant milk
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tbsp oil

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the dry ingredients.
  2. Pour the milk, water, and oil into the bowl. Stir until just combined. Overmixing will create less fluffy pancakes.
  3. Heat a pan and grease with vegan butter. Drop about ⅓ cup of batter into the pan for each pancake. Cook until both sides are golden brown.
