Eggy Tofu Scramble

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 block of tofu
  • 1 tbsp miso paste
  • 2 tbsp vegan yogurt, tahini, cream cheese or sour cream
  • ½ cup oat milk
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • ¼ tsp ground turmeric
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp onion powder
  • ¼ tsp paprika
  • ¼ tsp kala namak
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Into a heated pan with oil, crumble the tofu and let it fry off for a few minutes.
  2. Mix the remaining ingredients with a whisk and pour over the tofu. Let it bubble until the mixture has reached your desired consistency.
