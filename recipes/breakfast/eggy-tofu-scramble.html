Eggy Tofu Scramble

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • ½ a block of tofu
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • ½ tsp black salt
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Veggies of your choice

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, crumble the tofu and mix in the nutritional yeast, soy sauce, black salt, turmeric, and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté your veggies. Then add the tofu mixture and cook until heated through.
