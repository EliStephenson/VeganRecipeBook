Tiramisu Overnight Oats

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup rolled oats
  • 1 espresso shot or 2 tbsp strong cold brew
  • 1 tsp chia seeds
  • 1 tsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup
  • 3 tbsp plant milk
  • ¼ cup vegan yogurt
  • More sweetener and cocoa powder to taste for a topping

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients except yogurt in a jar or ramekin.
  2. Top the mixture with yogurt, sweetener, and cocoa powder.
  3. Refrigerate overnight.
