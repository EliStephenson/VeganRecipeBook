Very Easy Bagels

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 cup vegan yogurt
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • Melted butter
  • Toppings/Mix-ins:
  • Cinnamon and sugar
  • Everything but the bagel seasoning
  • Chocolate Chips

Instructions

  1. Mix together the dry ingredients then the yogurt.
  2. Flour a surface and shape the dough into 3 or 4 bagels.
  3. Pour on the melted butter and then add desired toppings.
  4. Bake at 375 degrees F for 20 minutes. These are yummiest fresh!
