Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups flour
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup unsweetened plant milk
  • 1 cup water

Instructions

  1. Whisk together the dry ingredients, then the wet.
  2. Use a ¼ cup to add batter to pan; immediately pick up the pan and swirl around the batter.
  3. Cook each crepe for about 2 minutes on the first side and 30 seconds on the other.
