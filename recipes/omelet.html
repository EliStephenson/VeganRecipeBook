Omelet

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 120 g firm tofu
  • 80 g silken tofu
  • 2 tbsp plant milk
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp kala namak
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 tbsp chickpea flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 sheets rice paper
  • Chili oil (or regular oil)
  • Vegan cheese
  • Optional: Spring onions, coriander, chives, vegan mayo, sriracha, and sesame seeds to serve

Instructions

  1. Blend all ingredients (up to the salt and pepper) until smooth.
  2. On a pan over medium heat, place a sheet of rice paper. Spread a small amount of oil on top, then pour and evenly spread half of the omelet mix. Cover and cook for 6 minutes. Sprinkle on cheese and herbs, fold in half, and cook for another minute.
  3. Serve with mayo, sriracha, sesame seeds, and any other desired toppings.
