No Bake Cookie Dough Bars

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 1 packed cup of soft medjool pitted dates
  • 2 ¾ cup rolled oats
  • ¼ cup tahini
  • 4 ½ tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • ½ tsp sea salt
  • 1 cup vegan chocolate chips
  • 2 tsp melted coconut oil

Instructions

  1. Blend the oats in a food processor to make flour.
  2. Add the dates, tahini, syrup, vanilla, and salt and blend again.
  3. Stir in half the chocolate chips.
  4. Line a pan with parchment paper and add the dough. Freeze for 20 minutes.
  5. Melt the chocolate chips with the coconut oil and add to the top of the dough.
