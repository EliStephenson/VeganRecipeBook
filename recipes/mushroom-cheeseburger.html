Mushroom Cheeseburger

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 4 portobello mushrooms
  • 2 tbsp vegan butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Handful of chopped parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 head of garlic
  • 60 g vegan grated cheese
  • 50 g vegan cream cheese
  • 80 g panko
  • 50 g + 20 g flour
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • ⅓ cup water
  • 2 burger buns, tomato, and greens to serve

  • Ingredients for Burger Sauce

  • 3 tbsp vegan mayo
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tsp dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp chopped pickles or pickle juice
  • ½ tsp onion powder
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Melt the butter and combine with garlic, parsley, salt and pepper. Brush your mushrooms with the butter mix, then bake for 40 minutes. Also bake your head of garlic (top cut off, wrapped in foil).
  2. Squeeze the roasted garlic out and mix with the cheeses. Make two sandwiches with a mushroom, the cheese, and the other mushroom.
  3. Place the panko in one bowl. In another mix 20 g flour, cornstarch, and water. In the last, add the remaining 50 g flour. Dip the mushrooms into the flour, then the wet mix, then the breadcrumbs. Repeat as needed.
  4. Fry your shrooms in oil over medium heat until crispy.
  5. Assemble your burger.
