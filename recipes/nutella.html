Nutella

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces hazelnuts
  • 2 ounces dark chocolate
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • ¼ cup plant milk
  • 1 ½ tbsp maple syrup

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Place the hazelnuts on a parchment-lined baking sheet and roast in the oven until lightly golden, 10 to 12 minutes.
  3. Wrap the hazelnuts in a thin kitchen towel and steam for 1 minute. Then, vigorously rub the nuts to remove the skins.
  4. Blend the hazelnuts in a food processor or blender for about 10 minutes or until you have a thick nut butter texture.
  5. Melt the chocolate in a microwave with the coconut oil.
  6. Add the melted chocolate, vanilla, milk, and maple syrup to the food processor and blend until well incorporated.
