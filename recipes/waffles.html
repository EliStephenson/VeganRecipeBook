Waffles

Back to Main Page

Ingredients

  • 200 g (7 oz) flour
  • 200 ml (0.8 cups) plant milk
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 50 mL (0.2 cups) water or sparkling water
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 tbsp oil or butter
  • 1 tsp lemon zest

Instructions

  1. Start by mixing all of the dry ingredients in a large bowl. Add the liquids little by little until the batter is smooth.
  2. Spray or spread waffle maker with neutral oil.
  3. Add a ladleful of batter to the waffle maker and bake until golden brown. Repeat until the batter is gone.
